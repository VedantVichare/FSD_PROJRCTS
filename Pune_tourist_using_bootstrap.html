Welcome to Pune

Discover the most popular tourist destinations in Pune.

Shaniwar Wada

Shaniwar Wada is a historical fortification in the city of Pune, built in 1732. It was the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire until 1818.

View on Google Maps
Aga Khan Palace

The Aga Khan Palace is a majestic building in Pune. It is one of the important landmarks in Indian history as it is closely linked to the Indian freedom movement.

View on Google Maps
Pataleshwar Cave Temple

Pataleshwar Cave Temple is a rock-cut cave temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. It was built in the 8th century in the Rashtrakuta period.

View on Google Maps
Sinhagad Fort

Sinhagad is a hill fortress located near Pune. It is situated on a hill rising 1312 meters above sea level. The fort has been the site of many important battles, most notably the Battle of Sinhagad in 1670.
